- **Check:** Verify if owner is renounced or a multisig

- Unlimited token creation
- Can devalue your holdings
- **Check:** Look for mint() functions without limits

- Functions that prevent selling
- Traps your investment
- **Check:** Analyze transfer/sell functions

**4. Centralized Control**
- Single point of failure
- Owner can pause/upgrade contract
- **Check:** Review access control modifiers

**5. Suspicious Liquidity Patterns**
- Liquidity not locked
- Low liquidity relative to market cap
- **Check:** Verify liquidity lock status
